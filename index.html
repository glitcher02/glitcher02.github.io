From an off-the-shelf CRM to bespoke tooling.

The problem

A luxury tour operator was running its sales on a generic CRM plus a sprawl of spreadsheets. Building a single proposal meant copying content by hand, and no one could see a trip's true margin until after it was sold.

What I built

A bespoke layer on top of their CRM: a drag-and-drop itinerary builder, live quoting with real-time margins, one-click branded proposals for clients, and an operational view for the team delivering each trip.

The result

Consultants build proposals in a fraction of the time, margins are visible before a trip is sold, and clients receive a polished, on-brand document every time — from the same data the ops team relies on.
